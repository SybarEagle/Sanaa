# Privacy Policy for Sanaah

Last updated: April 13, 2025

## Information We Collect
Sanaah collects your device location solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Location data is used on-device only and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

## How We Use Your Information
- **Location**: To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction for your current position
- **Notifications**: To deliver Azaan alerts at prayer times you have enabled
- **Local Storage**: Prayer tracker progress, Quran reading position, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only

## Data Sharing
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

## Third-Party Services
The app fetches prayer times from api.aladhan.com and Quran text/audio from api.alquran.cloud. These services receive your coordinates to return prayer times. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
